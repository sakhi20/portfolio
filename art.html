I paint what I feel.

SAKHI PATEL

I’ve always moved between two worlds — one built from data and logic, the other from pigment and instinct. Painting taught me to see before I understood. Every canvas is an attempt to slow down and stay with something longer than a glance.

I work mostly in acrylics and pastels. I’m drawn to portraiture, light on faces, and landscapes that feel like a held breath. These are some of the pieces I keep coming back to.
